Most productivity apps push you to do more, faster. GRDN asks a quieter question. What deserves your attention today, and how do you protect it?

What grows here

Everything you need to focus, nothing to distract you

GRDN brings together a gentle timer, a calm task list, and quiet accountability. The tools fade into the background so the work can come forward.

Focus sessions that hold

A Pomodoro-style timer pairs with iOS Screen Time to quietly block distracting apps while you work. Real friction against the impulse to switch away.

A to-do list like a garden

Group tasks into projects, set gentle priorities, and plant due dates only where they help. Tend a few things well instead of drowning in a hundred.

Quiet accountability

Start a session alongside friends and glance at weekly focus stats, a little like Apple Fitness. The presence of others motivates you. No feeds, no comments, no noise.

Gentle friction, not rigid rules

You can always stop a session. But ending early adds a deliberate pause, a small breath that turns an impulse into a choice. Intention over enforcement.

See your growth

Visual progress and honest analytics show your focus over days and weeks. Not a streak to anxiously protect, just a record of seasons tended.

Calm by design

Japanese-inspired sounds, a daily gratitude note, and Live Activities on your lock screen. Small touches that make focus feel like a place you want to be.

The gardening way

Productivity, reimagined as cultivation

You cannot force a plant to grow by pulling on it. The same is true of focus, of habits, of yourself. GRDN is built on patience.

Plant with intention

Each morning you choose what matters and let the rest wait. A garden is not measured by how many seeds you scatter, but by what you actually help grow. GRDN keeps your view small on purpose, so the important work stays in the light.

Protect the soil

Attention is the soil everything grows from, and the modern phone is designed to deplete it. During a session GRDN gently closes the doors that pull you away, so your focus has somewhere quiet to take root. The friction is small, but it changes everything.

Grow alongside others

Gardens flourish in community. With GRDN you can sit in focus next to friends and see the seasons they are tending too, without messages, likes, or anything to perform. Just the quiet knowledge that you are not doing this alone.
