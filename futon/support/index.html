Yonda: Read Real Japanese

Questions, bugs, or feedback: jeremydirons@gmail.com. We typically reply within a few days.

A scan failed or came out wrong

Scanning works best with a clear, well-lit, flat photo of the page. If a scan fails or the reading looks wrong, try re-taking the photo with better lighting and less glare/angle, then scan again. If it keeps happening, email us a description (and, if you can, the photo) and we'll take a look.

Privacy — what happens to my scans

Your scan photos are stored privately under your own account and are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to read the Japanese text — they're never made public and never used to train AI models. Full details: Privacy Policy.

Billing and subscriptions

  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store — we don't have access to your card details.
  • To manage or cancel a subscription: open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Yonda.
  • Refunds: requested directly through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com, or Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases: if you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, use the "Restore Purchases" option on the paywall/profile screen to reconnect a previous purchase to your account.

Deleting my account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account in the app. See Account Deletion for the full details, including what to do if you can't sign in.

Feature requests

We read every email. If there's a feature or content you'd like to see, send it to jeremydirons@gmail.com.
