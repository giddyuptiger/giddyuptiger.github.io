Cafe attach drives retention

Members who buy at the cafe weekly retain at 94% vs 71% for non-buyers — the cafe is a retention lever, not just revenue.

Trials convert through class 4

Trial members who hit 4+ classes convert to paid at 62%; those under 4 convert at 18%. Push new trials to their 4th class fast.

Lapsing members went quiet at the counter first

8 of the 22 members now 30+ days lapsed stopped buying cafe items 2 weeks before their last class — cafe silence is an early churn signal.

Top LTV members are dual-system

Every member in the top-10 LTV list is active in both class bookings and cafe spend. Single-system members cap out ~40% lower.
