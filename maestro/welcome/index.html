What it is

One calm place for everything you and Claude are running.

You talk to Claude. Claude does the work and writes it into Maestro. Maestro gives you a single, always-current view of it all — so nothing lives only in a chat history.

Get started

Up and running in three steps.

Create your account, connect Claude once, and watch your command center fill in.

1

Create your Maestro account

Sign up with an email and password. This creates your private, row-level-secured space — your data is visible only to you.

Create your account arrow_forward
mail You'll get a confirmation email — click the link to verify, then you're signed in. Use this same email and password when you connect Claude in step 2.
2

Add the Maestro connector in Claude

This is what lets Claude work in concert with Maestro — the write path. In Claude, open Settings and add Maestro as a custom connector:

Settingschevron_right Connectorschevron_right Add custom connector

Paste this URL, then click Add:

https://cixmyhdzzbgbltmitzkb.supabase.co/functions/v1/maestro-mcp
lock Claude will open a sign-in screen — sign in with the same Maestro account from step 1. That securely links Claude to your space over OAuth. Now Claude can read your overview and add projects, tasks, and notes for you.
3

Open your view and watch it fill in

Ask Claude to organize a project or track something — then open Maestro and sign in with your account to see it land in your overview, tasks, and calendar.
