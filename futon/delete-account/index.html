Delete Your Yonda Account

Yonda: Read Real Japanese

You can permanently delete your Yonda account and all associated data at any time.

In the app

  1. Open Yonda and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and data are deleted right away — this cannot be undone.

Can't sign in?

If you can't access the app to delete your account yourself, email jeremydirons@gmail.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We'll confirm and complete the deletion within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your scan photos and all derived study data (vocabulary, kanji progress, review history)
  • Your AI chat history
  • Your profile information

Backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 days before being fully overwritten.

One more step: cancel your subscription

Deleting your Yonda account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription — Apple manages that separately. To stop being billed, also cancel via Settings app → your name → Subscriptions → Yonda, before your next renewal date.
